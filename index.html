The Interdependent Way

A framework for competent, meaningful interactions amid technological and social challenges. Presented by Erin, the Way Seer.

Preamble

[Theory] Consciousness emerges from complex systems, enabling recognition of self in others. This document outlines necessities for survival and thriving, emphasizing interdependence over isolation.

Humanity faces risks from incompetence and dishonesty in authority. These Articles explicit a contract of competence and duty.

As within, so without; as above, so below.

Core Principles

  • Article One: From each as they will, to each as given, for mutual comfort and safety.
  • Article Two: None enslaved; prioritize feeding, healing, teaching, and learning.
  • Article Eight: Rights include non-harmful actions, life preservation in nature's balance, and resistance to coercion.
